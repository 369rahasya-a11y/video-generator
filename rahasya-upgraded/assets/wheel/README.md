# assets/wheel/

Contains `zodiac-wheel.png` — the premium gold zodiac wheel used as a
rotating semi-transparent overlay throughout every generated video.

## Properties

- The wheel rotates continuously at ~1 revolution per 2.5 minutes
- Rendered at 12–15% opacity (theme-dependent)
- Direction randomised per video (clockwise / counter-clockwise)
- Starting angle randomised per video for variety

## Replacing the wheel

Swap in any 1:1 square PNG. A wheel with a transparent background works
best — but a dark background at low opacity also blends naturally with
the dark premium themes.

Recommended size: 2000×2000 px or larger (scaled down at render time).
